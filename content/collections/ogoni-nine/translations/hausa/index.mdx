## Su Wanene Ogoni Tara?

Ogoni Tara ƙungiyar masu fafutuka tara ne masu murya da ƙarfi daga cikin ƴan asali — **Ken Saro-Wiwa**, **Barinem Kiobel**, **John Kpuinen**, **Baribor Bera**, **Felix Nuate**, **Paul Levula**, **Saturday Dobee**, **Nordu Eawo**, da **Daniel Gbokoo** — waɗanda suka soki lalacewar muhalli da ayyukan hako mai masu lalata a yankin Bakin Kogin Niger, waɗanda kamfanonin man fetur na ƙasashen waje suka jagoranta, musamman Royal Dutch Shell Company.

A matsayin wani ɓangare na gwagwarmayarsu ta lumana, sun kafa ƙungiyar adalcin muhalli da ake kira Movement for the Survival of the Ogoni People (MOSOP), wadda ta bayyana tasirin ibiribiri na hako mai kan rayuwa da rayuwar jama'ar Ogoniland, al'umma mai dogara da farko kan kamun kifi. Ƙungiyar ta ƙirƙiri Dokar Hakkin 'Yan Ogoni a watan Agusta na 1990, wani muhimmin takarda na tarihi wanda ya bayyana korafe-korafe, wahalhalu, da nufin 'yan Ogoni don ikon albarkatu, mulkin kai na siyasa, da kuma rabon dukiyar albarkatu.

## MOSOP da Dokar Hakkin 'Yan Ogoni

Bayan da suka kasa samun diyya ga 'yan Ogoni, MOSOP ta jagoranci jerin zanga-zangar da ta yi adawa da haɗin gwiwa tsakanin gwamnatin soja ta Najeriya ta Sani Abacha da kamfanonin mai na ƙasashe mabambanta. Fitaccen daga cikin waɗannan zanga-zangar shi ne Maci Ranar Ogoni — a ranar 4 ga Janairu, 1993, kimanin 'yan Ogoni 300,000 suka shirya babbar zanga-zanga da neman Shell ta daina ayyukanta da kuma ta bar Ogoniland. Aikin ya tilasta Shell ta jawo ma'aikatanta daga Ogoniland, kodayake lalacewar muhalli da ta bari ta ci gaba.

## Shari'ar Kotun Soja ta 1995 da Kisa

An kama Ogoni Tara a shekarar 1994 bisa zargin da ake yi musu na hannu a cikin mutuwar shugabannin Ogoni huɗu a wani taro na siyasa a Giokoo. An gudanar da shari'arsu a gaban wata kotun soja ta musamman wadda aka bayyana a matsayin ƙarya wanda ya yi ƙasa da ƙasa da ka'idojin ƙasashen duniya na shari'a ta adalci — kuma an fahimta sosai a matsayin hukunci saboda sukar da suka yi kan gwamnatin Abacha.

Kotun ta ƙi ba masu fafutukar damar yin ta'addanci da kuma damar daukaka ƙara ga hukumcinta. Ken Saro-Wiwa ya shirya wani jawabi don ya karanta a gaban kotun amma an hana shi yin haka. Ƙirƙirar wannan jawabi wani muhimmin ɓangare ne na wannan aikin ɗan adam na jama'a, wanda ke da nufin haskaka shaida ta sirri da aka murƙushe wadda in ba haka ba za ta ɓace daga tarihi.

An rataye Ogoni Tara a ranar 10 ga Nuwamba, 1995, a gidan yari na Port Harcourt. Kisan su ya haifar da fushi a duk faɗin duniya kuma ya haifar da dakatar da Najeriya daga ƙungiyar Commonwealth of Nations.

## Jawabin da aka Haramta

Kafin a kashe su, Ken Saro-Wiwa — marubuchi, shugaban MOSOP, da mafi shahararren daga cikin tara — ya shirya jawabi na mintuna shida wanda ya yi niyya ya karanta a gaban kotun soja ta musamman. Gwamnatin soja ta Sani Abacha ta hana shi karantar da shi.

Wani yanki na rubutun wannan jawabi yana akwai a Wikisource, ɗakin karatu na dijital na kan layi. Tun 1995, masu fafutuka da ƙungiyoyin farar hula sun shirya karanta jawabai bayan mutuwa a lokuta daban-daban. A shekarar 2020, ƙungiyar karatu mai tushe a Najeriya da aka sani da The Uyo Book Club ta shirya karatu na musamman don tunawa da cika shekara 25 na mutuwar Saro-Wiwa, wanda ya ƙunshi karatun sassan sanarwar da aka haramta.

## Yafiya ta Bayan Mutuwa da Buƙatun da Ake Yi Yanzu

Shekaru talatin bayan haka, Shugaban Najeriya Bola Tinubu ya ba Ogoni Tara afuwa ta shugaban ƙasa bayan mutuwa da kuma mutunci na ƙasa — wata yanke shawara da ta samu ra'ayoyi daban-daban daga membobin iyali da waɗanda suka fito daga Bakin Kogin Niger.

Ga yawancin mutane, ko da yake afuwar na maraba, ra'ayin afuwar kanta yana nufin cewa maza tara sun aikata wani laifi alhali gaskiyar lamarin ita ce ba su aikata kome ba. Masu sukar ra'ayin sun yi iƙirarin cewa kashe su wani rufewa ne kawai na haɗin gwiwar gwamnatin Abacha wajen lalata Ogoniland. Don haka, sun yi kira ga cikakkiyar tabbatar da 'yancinsu — wato sanarwar shari'a ta laifin da aka yi musu ba tare da son ransu ba — da kuma ƙoƙarin tsaftace muhalli na gaskiya ga Ogoniland, wata buƙata da gwamnatin Najeriya har yanzu ba ta biya ba.
