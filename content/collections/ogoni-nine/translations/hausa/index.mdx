## Waɗanda Ake Kira Ogoni Tara

A ranar 10 ga Nuwamba, 1995, gwamnatin soja ta Najeriya a ƙarƙashin Janar Sani Abacha ta kashe maza tara da suke fafutukar kare hakkin ƴancin 'yan Ogoni. Sunayensu su ne:

**Ken Saro-Wiwa**, **Saturday Dobee**, **Nordu Eawo**, **Daniel Gbooko**, **Paul Levura**, **Felix Nuate**, **Baribor Bera**, **Barinem Kiobel**, da **John Kpuinen**.

Gwamnati ta yi iƙirarin cewa sun kashe manyan 'yan Ogoni huɗu a watan Mayun 1994 — zargin da Amnesty International, Human Rights Watch, da kuma Bar Human Rights Committee na Ingila da Wales suka ce ba shi da tushe na gaskiya a fasahar shari'a. An toshe lauyoyin da suka wakilce su — Gani Fawehinmi da Oronto Douglas — daga ganawa da abokan hulɗarsu. Amnesty International, Majalisar Ɗinkin Duniya, Commonwealth, da gwamnatoci da yawa sun nemi a dakatar da kashe su. A safe na ranar 10 ga Nuwamba, 1995, an kashe su.

## Shell da Gurɓacewar Ƙasar Ogoniland

'Yan Ogoni suna zaune a ƙasar kilomita dubu ɗaya a cikin Bakin Kogin Niger, a kudu maso gabashin Najeriya. Tun daga shekarar 1958, Shell Petroleum Development Company — tana aiki a ƙarƙashin yarjejeniyar gwamnatin Najeriya — ta fitar da mai a yankin Ogoniland tsawon shekaru. Sakamakon haka shine lalacewar muhalli mai tsanani: ƙasar noma ta ƙonewa, koguna da tekuna sun ƙazantu, ruwan sha ya gurbace.

A rahoton Shirin Muhalli na Ƙungiyar Ƙasashen Duniya (UNEP) a shekarar 2011, an bayyana yankin Ogoniland a matsayin ɗaya daga cikin wuraren da man fetur ya fi gurɓata a duniya.

Ken Saro-Wiwa, marubuci kuma dan gwagwarmaya dan Najeriya, ya mai da fasaharsa wurin gwagwarmayar neman hakkin 'yan Ogoni. A shekarar 1990, ya kafa MOSOP — Ƙungiyar Tsaron Rayuwar Mutanen Ogoni — kuma ya tsara Dokar Hakkin 'yan Ogoni, wadda ta nemi ikon cin gashin kansu, adalcin muhalli, da kuma rabon kudaden mai ga 'yan Ogoni.

## MOSOP da Dokar Hakkin 'Yan Ogoni

MOSOP ta shirya ɗaya daga cikin manyan kamfe na lumana a tarihin Najeriya. A ranar 4 ga Janairu, 1993, kimanin 'yan Ogoni 300,000 — kusan rabin jama'arsu — sun taru a wani taron da aka san shi da "Ranar Ogoni", suna neman Shell da ta daina aiki da hakkin 'yan Ogoni. Shell daga baya ta janye ma'aikatanta daga Ogoniland, amma gurɓacewar muhalli ta ci gaba.

## Shari'ar 1995 da Kashe Rayuka

A watan Mayun 1994, an kashe manyan 'yan Ogoni huɗu a taron da aka yi a Giokoo. Ba Ken Saro-Wiwa a can. An kama shi da sauran mambobi tara na MOSOP, aka zargi su da kisan kai.

Kotun da ta yi shari'arsu gwamnatin soja ce ta zaba. Ta yi aiki ba tare da tabbacin kariyar da kotun farar hula ke bayarwa ba. An zabi masu saurare kan yanayin da ya dace. Masu kula da abin da ya faru a waje daga ciki sun yi tambaya game da halaucin aikata shi.

Ken Saro-Wiwa ya yi ƙoƙarin yin magana a gaban kotu. An hana shi. Jawabinsa da ya rubuta an kwato shi daga hannun danginsa kuma yanzu shine wani ɓangare na wannan archive.

A ranar 10 ga Nuwamba, 1995, an kashe su tara a gidan yari na Port Harcourt. Duk duniya ta ki yarda da kashe su. Najeriya ta fita daga Commonwealth. Amurka da Tarayyar Turai sun ɗora takunkumin da aka iyakance.

## Gafara da Neman Adalci

A shekarar 2024, Shugaban Najeriya Bola Ahmed Tinubu ya ba Ken Saro-Wiwa da sauran 'yan Ogoni takwas gafara bayan mutuwa. Dangin Saro-Wiwa da masu goyan bayansa sun karɓi sanarwar a matsayin farkon gane laifin da aka yi, kodayake mafi yawan masu fafutuka suna neman cikakken bincike — tambayar da ta rage ba a samu amsar ta ba — da kuma gyara hakika ga al'ummar Ogoniland.
