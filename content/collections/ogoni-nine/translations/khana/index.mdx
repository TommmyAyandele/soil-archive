## Khana Language Note

The Khana language (also rendered as Ogoni or Kana) is the primary indigenous language of the Ogoni people of Rivers State, Nigeria. It belongs to the Cross-River branch of the Niger-Congo language family. Ken Saro-Wiwa was himself a native Khana speaker who wrote extensively in English to reach international audiences, while working to preserve Ogoni indigenous languages. This translation is being developed in active consultation with Ogoni language scholars.

## Kọọ Nar Ogoni Ya Biakporum?

Biabari 10, Kọọ Nọvemba, 1995, olubu soja Nigeria nẹ Janar Sani Abacha sọlọ nar Ogoni biakporum nẹ gaa wii sụ doo. Nar ya bụ:

**Ken Saro-Wiwa**, **Saturday Dobee**, **Nordu Eawo**, **Daniel Gbooko**, **Paul Levura**, **Felix Nuate**, **Baribor Bera**, **Barinem Kiobel**, nẹ **John Kpuinen**.

Olubu sụ dọọ ya sọlọ nar Ogoni biee bẹẹ biakporum nẹ Kọọ Mee 1994 — kpẹẹ Amnesty International, Human Rights Watch nẹ Bar Human Rights Committee nẹ England nẹ Wales bẹẹ si nẹ ẹnẹ a ọ daa wii. Awọrọ ya, Gani Fawehinmi nẹ Oronto Douglas, a sị ya ạ ree nar ya ọbọ agọ ya. Amnesty International, Ụlọ obia Ụwa nile, Commonwealth, nẹ olubu orisiri sọ ka a kwụsị sọlọ ya. A sọlọ ya wii ụtụtụ 10 Nọvemba 1995.

## Shell nẹ Ibiri Ala Ogoni

Nar Ogoni bi nẹ kilomita puku ọta nẹ Niger Delta, ọwọwa na Nigeria. Site na 1958, Shell Petroleum Development Company — na-arụ ọrụ nẹ ikike olubu Nigeria — wepụtara mmanụ nẹ ala Ogoni ọtụtụ kụ. Ihe biri sị ẹnẹ a ya bụ ibiri gburugburu ebe obibi siri ike: ala ọhịa dọọ ada, ọdọ mmiri nke azụ dọọ ada, nẹ mmiri ọṅụṅụ biri ike.

Ọrọ na UNEP nẹ 2011 sụ ẹnẹ ala Ogoni bụ otu nẹ ẹnẹ a dọọ ibiri mmanụ kachasị njọ n'ụwa nile.

Ken Saro-Wiwa, onye ama ama nẹ onye dere akwụkwọ Nigeria, tụgharịa biri ya ka o bụrụ onye ọchịchọ ihe ọma maka nar Ogoni. Nẹ 1990, o mepụtara MOSOP — Movement for the Survival of the Ogoni People — wee detu Ogoni Bill of Rights nke chọrọ ikike onwe onye, ezi ikpe gburugburu ebe obibi, nẹ nkesa ego mmanụ maka nar Ogoni.

## MOSOP nẹ Ogoni Bill of Rights

MOSOP hazie otu nẹ ime ehe agọ udo kachasị nnọọ dị nẹ akụkọ ihe mere eme Nigeria. Nẹ 4 Janụwarị 1993, ihe dị ka nar Ogoni 300,000 — ihe dị ka ọkara nke nar nile — nọkọ nẹ ihe a a nọrọ ịkọ dị ka Ogoni Day, na-achọ ka Shell kwụsị ọrụ ya. Shell mechara wepu nar ya nẹ ala Ogoni, ma ibiri ahụ nọdụ ọrụ.

## Ikpe 1995 nẹ Igbu Ha

Nẹ Kọọ Mee 1994, a gburu nar Ogoni biee nẹ ọgbakọ nẹ Giokoo. Ken Saro-Wiwa adighi ebe ahụ. A tufuo ya nẹ ụlọ mkpọrọ, nẹ nar MOSOP biakporum ọzọ.

Ikpe nke lụrụ ha bụ nke olubu ndị agha họọrọ. O rụsịrị ọrụ na-enweghị nchekwa nke ikpe obodo nwere. Ndị na-ahụ maka ihe ka mma nke gara izute ikpe ahụ tụlere arụmụka maka ezi iziga ya.

Ken Saro-Wiwa kọọsịrị ọrọ maka ịkọ nẹ ụlọ ikpe ahụ. Enweghị ikike ya ọgụgụ ya. Edeetụọla ya e depụtara ya site nẹ aka ezinụlọ ya wee bụrụ akụkụ nke ndekọ a.

Nẹ 10 Nọvemba 1995, a gbuchie ha biakporum nile site nẹ ile ha nẹ ọgwụgwọ Port Harcourt. A jụ ụwa nile nẹ igbu ha. O dọghị Nigeria nẹ Commonwealth. United States nẹ European Union tọrọ ntọala ụfọdụ nkọwa.

## Ikike Echi Emechaghi Ikpe

Nẹ 2024, Onye isi ala Nigeria Bola Ahmed Tinubu nyere Ken Saro-Wiwa nẹ nar Ogoni biakporum ọzọ ikike echi emechaghi ikpe. Ezinụlọ Saro-Wiwa nẹ nar na-akwado ha anabatara nkwupụta ya dị ka mbido nkwupụta maka ajụjụ ahụ, o dị maka ọtụtụ nar na-akwado ya ka a chọọ ntọpụta zuru oke nẹ mmegharị ziri ezi maka obodo Ogoniland.
